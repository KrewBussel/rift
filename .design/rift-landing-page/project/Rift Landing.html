Why Rift

A structured pipeline where a thread used to be.

Spreadsheets track; Rift drives. Every case moves through seven disciplined stages, with checklists, tasks, and an audit log that nobody has to maintain by hand.

01 / PIPELINE
Seven stages. Zero ambiguity.
Intake → Awaiting Client → Ready → Submitted → Processing → In Transit → Complete. Advance a status, the checklist and activity log follow.
02 / INTELLIGENCE
Custodian intel that remembers.
25+ custodians mapped. Ask Claude how Schwab routes a Texas 401(k) — it cites your firm's own tribal knowledge and mailing rules.
